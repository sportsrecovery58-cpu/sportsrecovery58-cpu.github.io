Personal Information Collection

We collect phone number (including WhatsApp) when you book a session. This information is used for booking confirmations, session reminders, and promotional updates.

Use of Personal Information

Your contact details may be used to send you future promotions and offers. We do not share personal information with third parties except as required by law.

Security of Personal Information

All client information is stored securely. Entry to the premises is monitored via feed cameras for security and regulatory purposes; cameras do not record inside massage rooms.

Consent & Rights

By booking a session, you consent to the collection and use of your data as described. You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information at any time.

Booking & Payment Policy

Sessions can be 30 minutes or 1 hour, as selected during booking. A 500 PKR advance payment is required to confirm your booking. Cancellations made 1 Hour in advance will result in a full refund of the advance. Late arrivals may have session duration reduced, but rescheduling may be offered if feasible. No-shows will forfeit the advance payment unless prior notice is given.

Session Conduct & Professionalism

Clients must behave professionally during the session. This service is male-to-male only; Any harassment, abusive, or obscene behavior may result in immediate termination without refund.

Health & Safety

Clients must inform the therapist of any injuries, medical conditions, or allergies prior to the session. We reserve the right to refuse service if the session may pose a health risk.

Photography & Recording

Photography or recording inside the massage room is strictly prohibited. Personal belongings are the client’s responsibility.

Clients can opt-out of promotional messages at any time.

This service does not replace medical advice, and clients should consult a doctor for health concerns.

Dispute Resolution

Any disputes will be resolved amicably, and if necessary, in the courts of Multan, Pakistan.

Additional Conditions

Sessions are strictly 30 minutes or 1 hour; late arrivals will shorten the session time. Force majeure events (illness, emergencies, or natural events) may require rescheduling, and refunds will be handled as per the cancellation policy.
